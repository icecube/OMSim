\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Calculates muon starting position based on impact parameter to ensure the Cherenkov light cone hits the \doxylink{class_d_o_m}{DOM} at the origin.

Geometry\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item \doxylink{class_d_o_m}{DOM} at origin (0, 0, 0)
\item Muon travels in +x direction
\item Muon track at perpendicular distance r (impact parameter) in z
\item Starting x position\+: -\/1.\+7\texorpdfstring{$\ast$}{*}r (ensures Cherenkov cone reaches \doxylink{class_d_o_m}{DOM})
\end{DoxyItemize}

The factor 1.\+7 accounts for the Cherenkov angle in ice (\texorpdfstring{$\sim$}{\string~}41 deg)\+: tan(41°) ≈ 0.\+87, so light needs \texorpdfstring{$\sim$}{\string~}1.15\texorpdfstring{$\ast$}{*}r to reach \doxylink{class_d_o_m}{DOM} Using 1.\+7 provides margin for the full cone to illuminate the \doxylink{class_d_o_m}{DOM}.
